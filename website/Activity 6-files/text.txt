About

"Yohani de Silva, known professionally as Yohani, is a celebrated singer from Sri Lanka, renowned for her distinctive blend of Sinhala and English music. She gained widespread acclaim with the viral sensation "Manike Mage Hithe," where her enchanting voice and the song's infectious melody captivated audiences. Yohani has become a notable figure in the Sri Lankan music scene, showcasing her talent and contributing to the fusion of diverse musical styles."
